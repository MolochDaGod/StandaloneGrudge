How Heroes Work

Hero Creation

When starting the game, you choose a Race and Class. Each combination produces a unique hero with different stats, abilities, and racial traits. There are 6 races and 4 classes, giving 24 standard heroes plus 1 secret unlock.

Stats System

Each hero's stats are derived from a base template modified by both race and class bonuses. This means two warriors of different races will play quite differently.

Abilities

Each class has 4+ abilities shared across all races of that class. Abilities cost mana or stamina and range from passive bonuses to devastating ultimates. Class determines your playstyle.

Racial Traits

Each race provides passive traits that apply at all times. These stack with class abilities and can create powerful synergies (e.g., Orc Bloodrage + Worge Feral Rage).

Rarity & Difficulty

Rarity reflects how unique the hero's playstyle is. Difficulty rates how hard the hero is to master. Common/Uncommon are standard; Rare/Epic have unique mechanics; Legendary is top-tier complexity.

Factions

Heroes belong to one of 3 main factions which determines their visual theme and story alignment: Crusade, Fabled, or Legion. A hidden 4th faction, the Pirates, are neutral mercenaries who answer to no god — unlocked through secret characters.
