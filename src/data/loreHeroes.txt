THE WORLD OF GRUDGE WARLORDS:
The realm consists of endless floating islands drifting through an infinite sky.
At the edge of existence lies THE COSMIC WATERFALL - a void of pure entropy that
slowly consumes islands, pulling them into oblivion. The closer to the Waterfall,
the stronger the magic flows from the gods. The further away, the safer but weaker.
All races struggle between power and survival in this eternal migration.
